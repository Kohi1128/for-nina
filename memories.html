Memories

March

The beginning of everything. I still remember the first time we talked and hung out. It felt like I had known you for a long time. It was comforting in a way I didn’t expect.

April

It was the month I realized I had already fallen in love. The more I learned about you, the more my curiosity grew, and the more I found myself wanting to know every part of you.

May

A bittersweet month, but one that taught me so much. I’ll always be grateful for every moment we shared, even in saying goodbye.

June

Then we found our way back to each other, reconnecting to see where it might lead. With you, I found peace even in the uncertainty of tomorrow. No expectations. No pressure. Just gratitude for what we have today and excitement for whatever tomorrow may bring.
